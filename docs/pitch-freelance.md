# Freelance Pitch Pack

## Positioning

`go-service-template-pro` is a **client-facing industrial template** for launching reliable Go microservices with a built-in operations dashboard.

This is a demonstration of engineering quality, not a claim of certifications or years of experience.

## Why this reduces delivery risk for clients

1. Operational readiness is integrated from day one.
2. PR/CI governance enforces predictable quality checks.
3. Standardized architecture reduces onboarding and handover friction.
4. Logs/metrics/traces accelerate incident diagnosis.
5. Decision-maker-friendly UI improves progress transparency.
